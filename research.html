System logic

Each material choice is tied to a specific failure pathway.

The research begins from known limitations in zinc systems: passivation, dendritic zinc growth, cathode structural degradation, internal resistance, and electrolyte instability. The architecture responds with a scaffolded anode, a dual-active cathode, and a buffered gel electrolyte tuned for the electrode pair.

All figures below are research-model figures from the current specification. They should be read as electrode-stack estimates and validation targets, not finished commercial-cell guarantees.
